About Me

I received my BSc(Eng) degree from the Department of Information Security, University of Science Technology of China (USTC) in 2013 and my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2017. My PhD supervisor is Wing Cheong Lau who is the director of Mobitec Center in CUHK. I am the head of the Tianwen AI Department and currently in charge of the research and development of Security GPT at Sangfor Technology Co., Ltd..
